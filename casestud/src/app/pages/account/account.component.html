FAQs

What happens when I update my email address (or mobile number)?

When will my account be updated with new email (or mobile)?

After verification and saving changes.

What happens to my existing account?

Your account remains fully functional with order history and saved info.

Does seller account get affected?

Yes, changes reflect in seller account as well.
